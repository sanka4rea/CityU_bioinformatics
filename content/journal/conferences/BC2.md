<p align="justify">The Basel Computational Biology Conference (stylized as [BC]2) is a scientific meeting on the subjects of bioinformatics and computational biology. It covers a wide spectrum of disciplines, including bioinformatics, computational biology, genomics, computational structural biology, and systems biology. 

<img src="/img/journal/conf/BC2Basel.jpg" weight="500" Height="300" alt="bioinf" align=center />

<p align="justify">BC2 Basel Computational Biology conference is the scientific symposium of the SIB Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics, organized in collaboration with the Biozentrum University of Basel.
